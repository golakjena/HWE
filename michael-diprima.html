Michael Diprima

Senior Vice President

Senior Vice President Michael DiPrima joined Hodges Ward Elliott in 2005 and facilitates dispositions, financing assignments, valuations and other engagements. Michael has been involved in more than 500 hotel transactions totaling more than $17 billion and works alongside Partner and Senior Managing Director Mark Elliott.

In January 2015, Michael spearheaded the opening of HWE’s Los Angeles office where he manages the business development platform for the Western United States. Michael holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in International Business from Pepperdine University where he was a four-year letterman and captain of the nationally ranked Men’s Golf team. Michael resides in Atlanta with his wife and two children.
