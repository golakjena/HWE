Paul Gillen

Managing Director

Paul is an accomplished real estate professional with 20 years of experience that includes real estate sales, recapitalizations, acquisitions, financing and asset management. His experience covers all property types and investments throughout the capital stack and across the risk-reward spectrum. Paul has been involved in approximately $15 billion of completed commercial real estate transactions.

Prior to joining Hodges Ward Elliott, Paul has held officer positions at CBRE, Morgan Stanley, and UBS Realty. At CBRE, Paul oversaw all office transaction for its leading investment sales team in New York City and was a recipient of the Edward S. Gordon REBNY Ingenious Deal award in 2011. Before that, he was a Vice President in acquisitions at Morgan Stanley covering the MSREF and PRIME Property funds. He began his career at UBS Realty and holds an undergraduate degree from Georgetown University and an MBA from NYU.
