Todd Ratliff

Senior Vice President

Todd joined Hodges Ward Elliott in 2003 and spent his first five years with the firm selling focused service hotels and golf courses. In that five year period, he structured the sale of 33 hotels and three golf courses including three transactions with TIC sponsors in 2007 representing an aggregate value of nearly $500 million. Since 2008, Todd has focused his transaction expertise predominantly on full-service urban hotels, select-service portfolios and destination resort properties, completing transactions involving over 14,000 hotel rooms and an aggregate transaction volume in excess of $1.5 billion.

Prior to joining Hodges Ward Elliott, Todd served as a principal of an Atlanta-based commercial real estate information services company. He is a graduate of Clemson University with studies in Economics, Political Science and international studies in Spain.
