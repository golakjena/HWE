Nate Ries

Associate

Nate joined Hodges Ward Elliott in 2012. As a key member of CEO Bill Hodges' team, his chief responsibilities include hospitality industry analysis, marketing strategy and presentations, transaction management, and real estate market research. Prior to joining HWE, Nate worked as an analyst for an Atlanta-based consulting firm specializing in price optimization and revenue management for Fortune 500 companies.

Nate graduated from Duke University with a B.S. in Economics and a B.A. in History.
