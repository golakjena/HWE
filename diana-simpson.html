Diana Simpson

Assistant Vice President

Diana Simpson joined the team in 2015 as an Assistant Vice President in the Los Angeles office. Her primary responsibilities include business development and investment sales in the Western U.S. and Asia. Prior to joining HWE, Diana worked as an Analyst at LaSalle Hotel Properties, where she was responsible for asset management and financial analysis.

Diana graduated from Cornell University, where she earned a B.S. in Hotel Administration and a Minor in Real Estate.
