Rudy Reudelhuber

Managing Director

Rudy Reudelhuber is a Managing Director of Hodges Ward Elliott. He has managed $6 billion of transaction volume in the last five years and has generated over a $1 billion of transaction volume in the last twelve months. He joined Hodges Ward Elliott in 1998 and spent four years in London successfully establishing HVS Hodges Ward Elliott as a leading hotel broker in Europe, transacting over 20 hotels in 2007.

Rudy has led the execution of a wide variety of transactions in a wide array of locations. From the sale of the Manhattan Times Square Hotel, to the section 363 bankruptcy sale of the Doral Golf Resort & Spa in Miami, Florida, Rudy works to simplify the complexities of a transaction in order to achieve the optimal outcome for our clients.

Prior to joining Hodges Ward Elliott, Rudy was a Hong Kong-based assistant fund manager for global fund manager Trust Company of the West. Prior to that, Rudy was with the investment banking group of UBS Warburg in their New York offices. Rudy graduated with honors from Princeton and is a Fulbright Scholar.
