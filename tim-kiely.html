HEAD OF RESEARCH | DATA SCIENCE

Tim Kiely joined Hodges Ward Elliott in 2015 as Head of Research and Analytics. Prior to HWE, Tim worked as a Data Scientist at AIG's Commercial Property and Casualty business, and before that as a Consultant at Opera Solutions. An avid R programmer, Tim is a self-described data junky who enjoys competing in online data science competitions.

Tim received a BA from Williams College and is pursuing an MSc in Predictive Analytics from Northwestern University.
